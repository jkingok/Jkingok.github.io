That's what I told people at **Morning Startup**. This break from work I have really had fun *couch-coding*, *bed-coding *and *co-coding *(with Gemini).  
  
And I reached a bit of a milestone today. I'm leaving the "dipping toes" prototype era to having something I think I can throw a little weight behind.  
  
My little *Toga *Python programming system, heavily inspired by *Pyto* but happily operating under significant constraints is now "good enough".  
  
So I gave it some love and packaged it with its own name and icon, and I'm considering offering it up for *TestFlight*, when I figure that out.  
  
The elegance of Toga (thank you Russell and team) is from its constraint and simplicity - the number of widgets is limited but combining that with UIKit directly we get some pretty good results - I like my navigation stack UI with the *OptionContainer* as the anchor. I now feel happy for people to critique the code [on GitHub.](https://github.com/jkingok/mypystub)  
  
I have now implemented a couple of modules that operate within this environment, one I am really happy with but which is *completely bespoke to me*, but after all that is the point of this empowerment, is the one which helps me post blog articles.  
  
*Blog Master *takes this article that I write in Apple Notes then I convert it to Markdown, including any attachments, then adds the metadata required by *Jekyll* and a hero image. The file structure is then ready to go in *Working Copy* for a commit and push to *GitHub Pages. I haven't published this one to Git yet.*  
  
**Thus getting me back to the chance of posting more often. Here's to that...**
