I just posted about seeking industry engagements. It’s beneficial for these to be in person and to break old habits. The excuse of working in a remote area doesn’t hold any more.  
  
I trawled Meetup.com and in the past month I found something that it would have been great to know about *years ago*, and it has been running that long - **[Morning Startup](https://meetup.com/morning-startup-perth).**  
  
This fits a perfect niche of:  
- In Perth - itself perhaps a rarity  
- In the *morning, for those like me who have demands in the evening*  
- Near a train station - Elizabeth Quay with thanks to Riff by Spacecubed - *necessary because I literally barely made it to Kwinana before the freeway became a car park*  
- Inclusive in terms of attendance - no one minded that at this stage I’m only *startup-curious*  
- Inclusive in terms of speakers and content - I’ve only seen two so far but there’s an archive to dig into and it caters for self-nomination and you are given trust to deliver  
- You are left hydrated and refreshed thanks to their sponsors (a shoutout to Typeset!)  
  
All in all a bit of a unicorn itself really and I should point out, and this is a big one for me, globally inclusive. It’s time slot with the Perth time zone being what it is makes it something you could “tune into” as a professional development event in your organisation (hello New Zealanders at lunch).  
  
Happy to say I will be back, the crowd was great to me, either in person when I can make it and online if not. I think I’ll add it to my podcast queue…  
  
*Hot tip for networking though - bring a LinkedIn!*  
  
**Morning Startup Perth **is fortnightly by registration at Meetup.com or online at [PerthVideo.com.au.](https://perthvideo.com.au/morning-startup-perth)
